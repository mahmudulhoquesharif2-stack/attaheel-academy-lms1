# SECTION 01

## Core Design Philosophy

The website should never feel like a traditional website.

Instead, every page should feel like interacting with a beautifully sculpted premium object made from smooth organic liquid surfaces.

Every interface element must appear naturally connected to the overall composition.

Nothing should feel randomly placed.

Everything should emerge from the same visual language.

# SECTION 08

## Instructor Image Composition

The instructor portrait is not treated as a simple rectangular image.

Instead, it functions as the visual anchor of the landing page and appears as a naturally sculpted part of the interface.

The portrait exists inside a large organic liquid container inspired by slowly flowing cream.

It should never appear as a traditional photo inserted into a webpage.

---

## Upper Composition

The upper portion feels exceptionally clean and fresh.

Large continuous rounded corners eliminate every hard edge.

The portrait gently emerges from the background instead of sitting above it.

There is no visible border.

A subtle glass highlight follows the upper organic curvature.

The result feels open, calm, lightweight and premium.

---

## Side Composition

Generous negative space surrounds the portrait.

Small floating glass information cards hover beside the instructor.

They never touch the portrait.

Spacing remains consistent.

Floating badges create depth while preserving visual simplicity.

---

## Organic Portrait Frame

The portrait container behaves like liquid.

Its outline softly changes thickness.

Edges appear naturally sculpted instead of mathematically perfect.

The organic shape creates a handcrafted luxury appearance.

---

## Lower Transition

The lower section is intentionally unique.

The portrait does not end with a straight horizontal edge.

Instead, the image gradually dissolves into the interface.

Multiple visual layers create this transition:

Rounded cream surfaces

Semi-transparent glass sheets

Soft ambient shadows

Floating information capsules

Blurred organic overlays

Curved liquid geometry

The instructor image slowly disappears behind these layers.

Users cannot visually identify where the image ends and where the interface begins.

---

## Organic Integration

The bottom communicates one visual message:

"The interface grows naturally from the instructor."

The instructor clothing softly fades behind glass layers.

Curved cream surfaces continue downward.

Information cards overlap organically.

Subsequent content sections emerge naturally from this composition.

Visual Flow

Instructor Portrait

↓

Floating Glass Cards

↓

Organic Cream Surface

↓

Feature Section

↓

Course Information

without any harsh separation.

---

## Floating Depth

The portrait rests slightly above the background.

Very soft ambient shadows

Minimal elevation

Gentle reflections

Low opacity glass highlights

This creates premium depth without dramatic contrast.

---

## Overall Impression

The instructor portrait feels sculpted rather than inserted.

Fresh rounded top

Liquid inspired lower transition

Floating glass overlays

Together they create a calm, timeless, premium experience.
